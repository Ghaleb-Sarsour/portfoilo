What is Fencing?

Fencing is one of the oldest olympic sports in the world. In this sport, the main objective is to hit you opponent with your blade without getting hit yourself. There are three different weapons: Foil, Epee, and Saber. I play with the Epee weapon, which is usually considered the most tactical of the three weapons.
